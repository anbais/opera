Best Opera Houses in Kazakhstan

These are the places where many of the most important events in the history of Kazakh opera took place.

KSATOB

Kazakh State Academic Theatre

Constructed in 1930s, the building of the Kazakh State Academic Theatre of Opera and Ballet named after Abay is one of the most well-known sights of Almaty.

Considered one of the largest theaters in Eurasia, the KSATOB boasts an imperial exterior design combining the Italian classicism and traditional elements of the national architecture.

Astana Opera

Astana Opera

The opera house opened in Nursultan in 2013 with an inaugural performance of the Kazakh opera Birzhan and Sara.

It is considered the third biggest opera in the world. Initially inspired by the Italian theaters of the XIX century, the exterior design also includes elements of the national heritage, such as frescos of Sharyn Canyon and Burabay.
